“China condemns US sanctions on National People's Congress members, vows countermeasures”

[Beijing, Reuters] - On the 8th, the Chinese government condemned the US government's announcement on the 7th that it would impose economic sanctions on 14 members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC), China's legislature, and vowed to take countermeasures.

A senior official from China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated at a press conference, "China will take necessary measures and resolutely oppose the US's bullying."
